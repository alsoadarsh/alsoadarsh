# About Me
Hi, I’m Adarsh, a Business Analytics grad at the University of Dayton.  
Physics taught me to treat messy datasets like puzzles—if something looks off, I won’t rest until I figure it out.  

I thrive under last-minute pressure and enjoy polishing details until they’re just right—sometimes a bit too much—but it means the work I deliver is solid and reliable.  

I love projects that sit **between analysis and storytelling**: dashboards that get used, predictive models that actually inform decisions, or just staring at a CSV for an hour asking “who designed this?”  

Other passions: mentoring, teaching, making an impact in **education & healthcare**, and animals—dogs, cats, monkeys, snakes, spiders… you name it.
